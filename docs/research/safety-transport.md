# Madrid (Comunidad) — Safety + Public Transport (reference)

## Scope / caveats (important)
- **Granularity**: Official crime data is typically published at **municipio** or **distrito** level (not every *barrio*). Where barrio-level is unavailable, **safety score is inferred from district patterns + common local guidance** (Idealista/press) and should be treated as *directional*, not definitive.
- **CRIME SOURCES**: 
  - Ministerio del Interior: Portal Estadístico de Criminalidad (accessed but content blocked by cookie wall in this run; use interactively to pull district/municipality series).
  - **Numbeo (Madrid)** perceptions snapshot: Daylight walking safety **87.4/100**, night **70.0/100** (updated 8 Mar 2026).
- **TRANSPORT**: Metro/Cercanías coverage is stable, but station lists per barrio are long; this report focuses on **primary stations/lines** per area, plus **commute time ranges** to **Sol/Atocha**.
- **Zones**: CRTM tariff zones summary from Comunidad de Madrid site: **8 zones** (A, B1, B2, B3, C1, C2 + Castilla-La Mancha extensions). An abono for outer zone includes inner zones (e.g., B2 valid in A+B1+B2), except *interzonales*.

---

## Safety scale (1–5)
- **5** very safe: low-incidence/residential; low perceived risk.
- **4** safe: generally fine; normal city precautions.
- **3** average: mixed; depends street-by-street/time.
- **2** below average: higher theft/robbery incidence; more conflictive pockets.
- **1** avoid: persistent hotspots.

## Areas to avoid / be cautious (safety ≤2, or very pocketed)
- **Villaverde Bajo / San Cristóbal** (Madrid) — persistent hotspot reputation.
- **Parts of Usera** (Madrid) — varies a lot; be street-specific.
- **Parts of Puente de Vallecas** — especially deeper south/east pockets.
- **Some parts of Centro (Lavapiés/Embajadores)** — not “no-go”, but theft/harassment risk higher.

## Transport zones & Abono (what matters for budget)
- **Zones** (Comunidad de Madrid): **A, B1, B2, B3, C1, C2** (+ Castilla-La Mancha extensions). Outer-zone pass includes inner zones (e.g., **B2 includes A+B1+B2**), except special *interzonales*.
- **Pricing**: CRTM pricing page fetch failed during this run (404). Use CRTM official **Abono Transporte** page to pull current monthly costs (they change; also youth/senior discounts apply).

### Quick mental model
- **Zone A**: Madrid city + immediate core network.
- **B1**: first ring (e.g., Pozuelo, Tres Cantos, parts of Alcobendas/Coslada/Getafe).
- **B2/B3**: further suburbs (Majadahonda/Las Rozas often B2; Alcalá usually B3).
