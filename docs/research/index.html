Market trend

Direction: up. Not cooling in any meaningful way.

Main driver: demand is high, supply is constrained, regulation since 2019 reduced available rental stock.

Prime examples: Salamanca 2-bed ~€2,793, Chamberí ~€2,840, Pozuelo ~€3,600, Majadahonda ~€3,075.

Practical floor: even “cheap” commuter cities often sit at €1,000–€1,300 for a 2-bed.

Recommended approach

  1. Apply for Idealista API access immediately. Approval time is the bottleneck.
  2. Ship SSR wins first: Pisos.com, Yaencontre, Spotahome, Habitaclia.
